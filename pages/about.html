The Origin

From Skill Development
to Leadership Transformation

SkillTrain was founded in 2013 with a deeply human mission: to bridge the employability gap for India's underserved youth. We built vocational training institutes across Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and Rajasthan, and created one of India's largest free online vocational training channels on YouTube.

Over time, our work with organizations revealed a deeper truth — skills alone don't transform organizations. Leaders do. And so, SkillTrain pivoted to where the greatest leverage lies: leadership development, executive coaching, and people development for organizations that want to grow through their people.

Today, we work with startups, SMEs, large multinationals, and non-profits across the globe — bringing the same belief we've always held: invest in people, and everything else follows.
